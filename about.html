Yu Kakinoki is a billingual interdisciplinary designer, currently based in Tokyo, but often wandering somewhere in-between. The goal is to push the boundaries of digital storytelling. This website was built through GitHub Pages with self-learnt HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and the help of the Internet in my bedroom. My dissertation title was 'To what extent is the aura relevant to a work of art in the age of biocybernetic simulation?'
