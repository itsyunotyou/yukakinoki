Yu Kakinoki is a billingual art director, currently based in Tokyo. Have developed a distinct eye for aesthetics by growing up in California, Japan, Hong Kong, and London. The goal is to push the boundaries of storytelling through spatial design - whether it be a tangible room, a digital space, or both. This website was built through GitHub Pages with self-learnt HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Bootstrap. Dissertation title was 'To what extent is the aura relevant to a work of art in the age of biocybernetic simulation?'
